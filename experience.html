The experience

This isn't dinner. it's an Argentine afternoon.

An asado is a slow social event built around the grill — typically 4 to 6 hours, starting with chorizo and finishing with coffee and a digestif. Here's what happens, hour by hour.

–1h

We arrive & set up

Grill, charcoal, tables, glassware, condiments — quietly, before the guests show up.

00

Picada & first wines

Cheese, salame, olives, provoleta on the grill, a glass of Malbec in hand.

+45m

Chorizo & morcilla

The first round comes off the fire — chorizo, morcilla, sweetbreads if you want them.

+1h30

The cuts arrive

Vacío, entraña, asado de tira, picaña — sliced at the table, served in waves.

+3h

Dessert & coffee

Flan with dulce de leche, espresso, maybe a fernet to close.

+4h

We pack up

Grill, embers, plates — everything leaves with us. No cleanup on your end.

Where we set up

Your place. or a place we find for you.

Option A

Your Airbnb or rental

If your place has a terrace, garden, rooftop or pool area, we bring the asado to you. We work cleanly and respect house rules.

Option B

Your home in Barcelona

For locals and expats with a balcony or patio. We adapt to small spaces — the parrilla can run on a 2 × 1 m terrace.

Option C

A venue we book for you

No outdoor space? We'll find and reserve a suitable venue — rooftop in Eixample, masía in Penedès, beach plot in Castelldefels. Cost is added to the quote.

How booking works

Four steps. no friction.

Step 1

You send the basics

Date, number of guests, where you're staying. A line about what you want — chill family lunch, anniversary, bachelor party.

Step 2

We send a quote

Within 24 hours. One per-person price, a suggested menu, and a venue plan if you need one. We adjust until it fits.

Step 3

You confirm w/ deposit

30% to lock the date. Final balance the day of the event. We accept transfer, Bizum, cash or card.

Step 4

We show up & cook

You open the door. We do the rest. Your only job is to sit at the table.

Frequently asked

Things travelers ask us.

How far in advance should we book?
Two weeks is comfortable. We can sometimes do 3–5 days, weather and availability permitting.
Do you cater to vegetarians?
Yes — provoleta, grilled vegetables, mozzarella & tomato, salads. Tell us how many in your party.
Can we choose the cuts?
Absolutely. Tell us what you've tried, what you want to try, and we'll build the menu around it.
What if it rains?
We reschedule at no charge with 24h notice, or move to a covered venue. Your deposit doesn't expire.
Do you speak English?
Yes — fluent. We also speak Spanish and Italian. The whole experience runs in English by default.
Is gratuity included?
No. Tipping is not customary in Spain. If you loved the experience, tell your friends.
