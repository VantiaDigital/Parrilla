The Experience

This isn't dinner. It's an Argentine afternoon.

An asado is a slow social event built around the grill — typically 4 to 6 hours, starting with chorizo and finishing with coffee and a digestif. Below is what to expect, hour by hour, when you book us.

  1. –01H We arrive & set up parrilla, charcoal, tables, disposable tableware, sauces — quietly, before the guests show up
  2. 00H Cold drinks open ice tubs go out, cans cracked open — beer, soft drinks, canned cocktails. The fire is lit.
  3. +45M Vegetables on the embers vegetable skewers and whole homemade vegetables go on first — peppers, onion, zucchini, eggplant
  4. +1H30 The cuts arrive vacío, entraña, asado de tira — sliced at the table, served in waves with bread, chimichurri and salsa criolla
  5. +3H Long table seconds, thirds, cold cans still flowing — the Argentine part where nobody gets up for a while
  6. +4H We pack up parrilla, embers, every disposable plate and can — everything leaves with us. No cleanup on your end

How booking works

Four steps, no friction.

Step 1

You send the basics

Date, number of guests, where you're staying. A line about what you want — chill family lunch, anniversary, bachelor party.

Step 2

We send a quote

Within 24 hours. One per-person price, a suggested menu, and a venue plan if you need one. We adjust until it fits.

Step 3

You confirm with a deposit

30% to lock the date. Final balance the day of the event. We accept transfer, Bizum, cash or card.

Step 4

We show up & cook

You open the door. We do the rest. Your only job is to sit at the table.

Frequently asked

Things travelers ask us.

  • How far in advance should we book? Two weeks is comfortable. We can sometimes do 3–5 days, weather and availability permitting.
  • Do you cater to vegetarians? Yes — provoleta, grilled vegetables, mozzarella & tomato, salads. Tell us how many in your party.
  • Can we choose the cuts? Absolutely. Tell us what you've tried, what you want to try, and we'll build the menu around it.
  • What if it rains? We reschedule at no charge with 24h notice, or move to a covered venue. Your deposit doesn't expire.
  • Do you speak English? Yes — fluent. We also speak Spanish and Italian. The whole experience runs in English by default.
  • Is gratuity included? No. Tipping is not customary in Spain. If you loved the experience, tell your friends.
