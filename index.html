Why book with us

An Argentine ritual,
without the planning.

You bring the friends. We bring everything else — including the venue, if you need one. No shopping list, no scrambling for charcoal, no setup or cleanup. Just the long table you came here for.

01

Meat & fire

Argentine cuts grilled the way they were meant to be — slow, over real charcoal, by people who grew up doing it. Chorizo, morcilla, entraña, vacío, asado de tira, picaña.

02

Drinks included

Argentine Malbec, local wine, beer, soft drinks and water. We pair the table — you don't have to think about the cellar.

03

Venue if you need it

Staying in an Airbnb without a terrace? We'll find and book a suitable spot in Barcelona — rooftop, garden, masía — and handle the whole arrangement.

04

One price, no surprises

A single per-person quote covers food, drink, equipment, charcoal, service and cleanup. No add-ons, no last-minute extras.

The brothers

Bruno & Facu.
One grill, since forever.

We're two brothers from Argentina, now living in Barcelona. Back home, Sunday meant an asado — neighbours, cousins, music, hours at the table. That's the experience we bring to your event.

Not a buffet, not a food-truck. A proper Argentine parrilla, hosted by people who care about how the chorizo is bitten before the entraña hits the grill.
