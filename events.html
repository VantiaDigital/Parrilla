Events & private parties

Two guests, or a hundred. Same fire.

We host everything from intimate balcony lunches for travelers to full wedding receptions in Empordà. Below are the formats we handle most often. None of them are off the menu — and we'll build a custom plan for anything else.

For travelers

Airbnb Asado

The most-booked format. You and your friends rented a place with a terrace — we bring the whole Argentine afternoon to it.

  • 4 to 12 guests
  • 4 hours, end to end
  • We arrive 90 minutes before
  • Bilingual hosts (English & Spanish)
  • Cleanup included
Milestones

Birthdays

For 30ths, 40ths, 50ths and any reason to gather a table. A long lunch beats a restaurant booking.

  • 10 to 40 guests
  • Cake & candles on request
  • Playlist & speaker available
  • Custom menu & pairing
  • Photo of the table at the end
For groups

Bachelor / Bachelorette

The original group-bonding event. Asado replaces the booked-out restaurant; you stay where you are, drink at your pace.

  • 8 to 25 guests
  • Welcome cocktail option
  • Cigar pairing add-on
  • Late finish if needed
  • Privacy: it's your house
The big one

Weddings

An Argentine wedding asado is a thing. Long tables, slow service, late afternoon into the night. We coordinate with your planner.

  • Up to 100 guests
  • Full service team
  • Venue scouting in Empordà / Penedès
  • Custom menu & vegetarian alternatives
  • Welcome drinks & late dessert
Corporate

Company retreats

For teams in Barcelona for a week, or a local company doing year-end. Conversation over a 4-hour grill beats a conference room dinner.

  • 15 to 80 guests
  • Invoiced with full VAT receipt
  • Dietary requirements handled
  • Venue with AV if needed
  • Closing speech welcome
Anything else

Private dinners & surprises

Engagement proposals, anniversaries, family reunions, a Sunday lunch to settle a debate over wine. If a grill fits, we'll be there.

  • From 2 guests
  • Sommelier add-on
  • Flowers & table styling on request
  • Discreet service
  • NDA available for press / VIP
Weddings in detail

An Argentine wedding,
under Catalan sky.

For couples who don't want a hotel reception. We've cooked for weddings in Penedès masías, beach plots in Garraf, country houses in Empordà, rooftops in Eixample.

For larger events

What we need from you.

For groups of 30+, planning is part of the deliverable. Here's the minimum we need to send a quote.

01

Date & rough headcount

Even a window helps — "first Saturday in June, around 60 guests." We'll lock numbers later.

02

Location or willingness

If you have a venue, share the address. If not, tell us your area (Barcelona / coast / inland) and your indicative budget for it.

03

Dietary & alcohol notes

Vegetarian count, allergies, dry crowd vs wine-heavy crowd, kids in the mix — anything we should plan around.

04

The vibe you want

Formal & seated? Casual & standing? A welcome cocktail before the meat? A late-night fernet bar? Tell us the day you're picturing.
